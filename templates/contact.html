Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats are supported?

Currently, our system supports .wav audio files. We're working on adding support for additional formats like .mp3 and .ogg in future updates.

How accurate is the detection?

Our system achieves over 95% accuracy in detecting deepfake audio. However, accuracy may vary depending on the quality and type of audio being analyzed.

Is my audio data stored?

No, we do not store any uploaded audio files. All processing is done in memory, and files are automatically deleted after analysis.

Can I integrate this into my application?

Yes! We offer API access for developers who want to integrate our detection technology into their own applications. Contact us for more details.

What's the maximum file size?

Currently, we support audio files up to 10MB in size. For larger files, please contact us to discuss enterprise solutions.

How long does analysis take?

Most audio files are analyzed within 2-5 seconds, depending on the file size and current server load.
